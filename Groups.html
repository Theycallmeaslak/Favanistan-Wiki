Exalia

Just like Youn, Exalia is also a world spanning empire, although it is slightly smaller, it has proven time and time again that it is not to be regarderd as Youn's inferior. Exalias citizens are mainly elves, most of which are high elves. They recently declared war on Youn, to exploit an advantage granted to them by the mythical metal IX-25. They are known to recruit women, and many divisions are partly or entierly female. This is done to reduce the overall barbarity of military action, which in turn allows military action to a bit more discrete. A trail of burned villages and destruction behind it everywhere is the last thing an empire needs, particularly if it's continued exsistance relies on some general idea of imagined superiority and civility. This is low key France, a counterpart to the obvious British empire, although way more evil.

Favans Demons

After expelling the mortal races from Valisia, wiping their memories and making it inhospitable, Favan sent an entire entourage of servants and soilders to protect and take care of lady Elia. They have on occasion come to blows with The first cohort and other valisisan natives that still remained in Valisisa Due to pressure from the invaders from both Youn and Exalia, the Demons in Valisia are nothing but remnants of their former selves, and they have failed in their one mission, to protect lady Elia. It is unknown what will happen to them now, but it Favan is not known to revard failure.

The First Cohort

An anicent nation. It was destroyed by Demon king Favan when he rid valisia of mortals. However, the first cohort was a rough place that fostered resentment, anger, bitterness and rage. As such a considerable poriton of the spirits could not find peace, and now haunt various fortresses in the area they used to live. These warriors seem to not fully understand that they are dead, nor do they seem to grasp how long time has gone by. Their conciousness too must have been affected by the spell Favan used to wipe the memories of the mortals of valisia. They seem to remember their animosity towards the demons though, and are open to cooperate with anyone in valisia willing to fight for the common goal of removing the demons from the land. Led by the man that originally founded the nation, and their first mighty general-king, Cai Junnian. They might yet become a force to be reckoned with.
